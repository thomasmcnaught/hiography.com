Quid Pro Quo

Apparently, one of my posts was so interesting that you're now contemplating exchanging personal information for a monthly summary of any posts. Alternatively, you may have landed here by mistake because you've been taught to click colourful links when you see them without understanding the consequence.

I can go a while without posting, then suddenly there are six updates overnight. It's unreasonable to expect you to visit every day—filled with hope—only to be gutted when you see nothing new. Instead, I'm offering a monthly newsletter that will highlight anything you may have missed while you've been doing important things like being mentally stable.

The newsletter will be delivered to your inbox on the last day of each month… or maybe the first day of the proceeding month. Should you find yourself lacking a basic understanding of mathematics, that's twelve emails throughout 2022. Pretty reasonable, IMO.
