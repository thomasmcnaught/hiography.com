Plausible Analytics

2021-10-24T04:50

Before I fall asleep, I'm integrating Plausible Analytics to the site as I'm curious to know if anyone is interested in a stupid art project I want to explore.

I've opted for Plausible as a privacy-friendly alternative to Google; it doesn't track you, sell your data, or listen to your private conversations so they can conveniently show you where to purchase the product you were just talking about, even if I do find it handy sometimes because I'm very forgetful. You'll notice I also use ProtonMail as my email provider instead of Google.
